About Me

Hello! I'm Yiyang Bai, a Data Science student at Northeastern University. I am passionate about Machine Learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI-driven financial research. My journey in data science began with a curiosity about how data can drive decisions, leading me to explore various fields such as neural networks, database design, and algorithmic structures.

I have gained hands-on experience through research at Tsinghua University AI x Lab, where I worked on LLMs, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and agent systems. Additionally, I conducted biotech research at Zhengzhou University, focusing on immune cell analysis. My projects include developing a Breast Cancer Diagnosis System using LSTM and XGBoost and an LLM-powered investment research automation system.

Beyond academics, I enjoy golf, kickboxing, and playing the trombone. These activities help me stay disciplined and provide a creative balance to my technical work. My goal is to leverage AI and machine learning to solve real-world problems, particularly in finance and healthcare.This website serves as a platform to showcase my projects, share insights, and connect with like-minded individuals. Thanks for visiting, and I hope you find something interesting here!
